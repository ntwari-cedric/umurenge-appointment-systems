Welcome to the Umurenge Appointment System

A digital platform that helps citizens book public service appointments, receive updates, and save time when visiting their Umurenge offices.

Scroll down to learn more

Why Use This System?

The system is designed to improve service delivery, reduce waiting time, and allow citizens to interact with Umurenge offices more efficiently.

Easy Booking

Users can select a service, choose an Umurenge office, and pick a suitable date starting from tomorrow.

Track Updates

Citizens can log in anytime and see whether their appointment was approved, rejected, shifted, or cancelled.

Faster Service

Instead of waiting in long physical lines, users prepare their visits in advance and arrive with a confirmed request.

How It Works

The process is simple and designed for both citizens and administrators.

01

Create Account

A citizen first creates an account through the sign-up page and then logs into the system.

02

Book Appointment

The user selects an office, chooses a service, writes a description, and selects a future date.

03

Office Review

The Umurenge office dashboard shows all requests sorted by date, where officers can approve or reject them.

04

Get Updates

The user sees the latest status at any time and can also cancel or shift an appointment when necessary.

Who Uses the System?

The platform supports three main roles in the appointment workflow.

User

Registers an account, books appointments, checks updates, cancels appointments, and shifts dates when needed.

Umurenge Office

Views requests from users, opens appointment details, and makes decisions by approving or rejecting requests.

Admin

Manages system setup by adding Umurenge offices, adding services, deleting records when necessary, and checking database users.
